Promote

As a promoted member yourself, you have the ability to promote/demote other members(including {{messageMember[1]}}!) of the group-chat. The perks of being a promoted member include: being able to edit the conversation title & add/delete members. One can become a promoted member by one of two ways: being the initiator of the earliest non-deleted message(or of the message whose sender has not left the group-chat), or by being given the privilege by some other promoted member in the same group-chat. Any promoted user that leaves the left the chat, will not gain automatic promotion upon rejoining; they will instead need to be given that privilege by one of the already promoted users.
